About Me

Hi, I am Vineeta, a healthcare administrative professional transitioning into data analysis. My passion is finding stories within data to drive better outcomes. In my current role, I find creative solutions to accommodate last minute schedule changes,resolving conflicts between multiple surgeon availability and addressing patients' concerns and requests.

Passion to make a difference in patients' lives brought me to healthcare while desire to improve healthcare quality, efficiency and accessibility on a larger scale has led me to pursue a career in data analysis. Throughout my career, I have managed various administrative functions such as scheduling, billing and patient record management while ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. I bring a blend of healthcare expertise and data analysis skills. Data analysis can lead to better patient outcomes by identifying trends in patients health, predicting disease outbreaks and optimizing treatment protocols. With a strong foundation in healthcare operations, compliance and patient care along with proficiency in SQL, Python and Data Visualization tools, I am determined to bring meaningful change across industries.

Check out my portfolio of projects. I will walk you through different business cases where I show the process of cleaning and exploring data using statistics to reveal hidden insights.
